Trust Boundaries

About This Model

Trust boundaries define where you verify identity, enforce policies, and monitor behavior. In traditional perimeter security, trust was binary: inside = trusted, outside = untrusted. Modern zero-trust architecture recognizes that trust is a spectrum, and verification must happen continuously at every boundary.
